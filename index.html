Songo

Songo is a simple songwriting lyric and chord discovery tool. It contains over 100,000 words and features a full thesaurus, rhyming dictionary and idioms database and includes over 1000 chord charts. Chords are arranged by major and minor keys along with related complimentary chords to add a little sauce to your mix and the entire database (both lyric and chord) is local, therefore no internet connection is needed.
